Behavior & Management of Domestic Species (ANSC 404)

Syllabus:

Feedyard employee attitude survey
  • Stockperson perception and attitude is a predictor of stockperson behavior. Stockperson behavior has a direct impact of animal productivity and welfare.
Fear test and emotional evaluations in cattle
  • Pilot: Preliminary evaluation of cattle behavior in novel test to quantify anxiety in cattle
  • Study 1: Comprehensive evaluation of cattle behavior in multiple fear tests
  • Study 2: Relationship between fear test behavior and latency for heart rate to return to baseline
Social mixing of beef cattle
  • Study 1: Impact of rapid repeated mixing of replacement heifers on productivity, behavior, and rebreeding rates
  • Study 2: Impact of repeated mixing of backgrounded steers on productivity, behavior, and health
  • Study 3: Impact of a terminal sort on cattle aggression
Environmental enrichment
  • Interaction between environmental enrichment and roughage on cattle behavior, health, and productivity
Tongue rolling and acidosis
  • Can tongue rolling behavior be a behavioral indicator of sub-acute acidosis?
  • Study 1:Evaluated from cattle fed different roughages
  • Study 2:Evaluated from cattle experiencing a SARA challenge
  • Study 3:Development of a model illustrating the relationship between tongue rolling, mastication, rumination, and rumen pH
Feedyard welfare metric library
  • Comprehensive evaluation of feedyard operations and how management impacts cattle welfare.
  • Spent 10 days working as a feedyard employee to gain context regarding what realistic expectations are for a practical on-farm feedyard assessment.
  • Developed library of metrics that can be used to objectively evaluate fed cattle welfare
Product testing for Aversion Technologies, Inc.
  • Evaluate the efficacy of different plastic additives on wire damage from rodent chewing
  • Study 1:Ford/Coroplast wire harness test-2
  • Study 2:Efficacy of R4R and R4R+ on wire chewing behavior of rats
  • Find out more about Aversion Technologies Here
Exercise to enhance cattle welfare
  • Study 1:Empirical evaluation of different exercise regimes on weanling cattle welfare
  • Study 2:Empirical evaluation of different exercise regimes on high risk receiving cattle welfare
Social grooming behavior
  • Investigation into the usefulness of allogrooming behavior as a tool to detect sick cattle
