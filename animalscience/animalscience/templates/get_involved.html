Animal Welfare Club

The Animal Welfare and Behavior Club is a student organization that aims to promote awareness and education of animal welfare to Texas A&M University and the greater community. The AWBC provides students with opportunities to interact with professionals in the field of animal welfare and gain animal experience through volunteer service. Each semester we will be hosting presentations, guest speakers, mock welfare judging competitions, and educational trips.

We are open to all undergraduate students, graduate students, faculty, and staff members. Those who are interested in the Animal Welfare and Behavior Club should contact Xandra Meneses at presidenttamuawbc@gmail.com or Kayla Cates at vptamuawbc@gmail.com
